Our Services

Since 1999, we and our team has focused on how we can teach with passionately. We want our learners to be active instead of being passive. That's why, our courses have some sections according to the ability of students. They do not need to just listen a video and then take a quiz. They need to work with eachother, they should create some projects, and they are also expected to present their works to help them improve their social skills. In a nutshell, we have:

  1. Videos to prepare learners

  2. Activities to make them active learner

  3. Projects to entegrate their knowledge into real life

  4. Presentation days to gain inspiration from each other
